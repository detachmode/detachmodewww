Maxscript

INSERT BEZIER KEY

Adding a key to an animation curve of a bezier controller without changing its shape.



Download

Installation:
1. Drag and drop into 3dsMax.
2. Go to Customize->Detachmode->InsertBezierKey and drag and drop it to the menubar.

Usage:
1. Open Trackview and select one or more bezier controllers and go to the frame where you want to add a key to the curve.
2. Start the script and press the button.
